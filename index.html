Welcome

This resource has been developed to provide AFL players with practical, evidence-based nutrition information to support training, performance, and recovery. The content has been shaped by feedback from current AFL players to ensure it addresses the real challenges athletes face.

"Real examples of what other athletes do to give a guide, and with reasoning as to why they eat/drink certain things." - AFL Half Back Flank, on what would make a nutrition website useful

Key Challenges Identified by AFL Players

Through our survey of AFL players, several common nutrition challenges emerged:

Time Constraints

Busy training schedules and club commitments make meal preparation difficult, especially when combined with fatigue.

Travel & Away Games

Interstate travel disrupts routines and limits food options - identified as a key challenge by surveyed players.

Club Culture & Overeating

"Lots of food is provided, so it is often easy to overeat" - balancing team events with nutrition goals.

Knowledge Gaps

Players reported confusion around the role of fats and calculating caloric intake based on physical output.
